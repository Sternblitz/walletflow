## üì± 5. Apple Wallet Best Practices

### 5.1 Pass Types
- **storeCard:** 375x144pt Strip (Standard)
- **coupon:** 375x144pt Strip
- **eventTicket:** 375x98pt Strip
- **generic:** 375x123pt Strip

### 5.2 Image Requirements
- **Icon:** 87x87px @2x (174x174px)
- **Logo:** 160x50px @2x (320x100px)
- **Strip:** 1125x432px @3x (Store Card)
- **Format:** PNG with Transparency

### 5.3 Field Limitations
- **Header Fields:** 1 (Top Right)
- **Primary Fields:** 1 (Overlay on Strip)
- **Secondary Fields:** 2 (Below Strip)
- **Auxiliary Fields:** 1 (Bottom)
- **Back Fields:** Unlimited (Back of Pass)

### 5.4 Color Guidelines
- **High Contrast:** Text must be readable
- **Dark Mode Support:** Test both Light/Dark
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA Compliance
